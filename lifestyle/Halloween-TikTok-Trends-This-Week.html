Halloween Top TikTok Trends This Week




Hot Club Tracks Staff - October 28th, 2024


Halloween Top TikTok Trends This Week

1. #SpookySeason Glow-Ups

With Halloween just around the corner, TikTok users are jumping into the spooky spirit with creative costume transformations. The #SpookySeason trend has brought back popular makeup and costume tutorials, where users show impressive transitions from everyday looks to eerie, spine-chilling characters. From “Vampire Diaries”-inspired makeovers to classic ghosts and witches, these videos are racking up millions of views.

2. #TinderTransparency

The dating app world is buzzing with a new trend as users share their dating profiles and experiences with the hashtag #TinderTransparency. This trend highlights funny, relatable, or awkward interactions and offers tips for setting up profiles that stand out. It’s both a humorous and enlightening look into the modern dating scene, with users sharing what works and what doesn’t when swiping.

3. Cozy Fall Recipes

Food trends on TikTok are never far behind, and this season, cozy fall recipes are taking over. Users are sharing their favorite recipes for pumpkin spice lattes, homemade apple pie, and comforting soups. These videos not only provide great ideas for seasonal dishes but also showcase beautiful visuals of fall-inspired ingredients and presentation.

4. Halloween Costume Ideas for Pets

This trend might be the cutest yet! Pet owners are creating short videos featuring their pets in Halloween costumes, ranging from adorable pumpkins to mini superheroes. The #PetCostume trend is full of light-hearted, feel-good content that’s bringing plenty of smiles and gathering lots of shares and comments.

5. New Music Challenges

New music challenges have emerged as various artists drop singles perfect for TikTok dance routines and lip-syncing. Tracks like “single choice” by [popular artist] have inspired choreographed dances, while trending songs from past decades are being revived as TikTok users add their twist to the classics.

Top Story: Taylor Swift's '1989 (Taylor's Version)' Release

This week, the top story dominating TikTok is Taylor Swift's re-release of her iconic album 1989, dubbed "1989 (Taylor's Version)." Fans are celebrating the release by creating content inspired by the album’s songs, particularly focusing on her newly re-recorded tracks and the nostalgia of her classic pop anthems. The #1989TaylorsVersion hashtag is filled with reactions, fan theories, and celebratory posts, with Swifties sharing their favorite songs, lyrics, and even Taylor-inspired outfit ideas.
