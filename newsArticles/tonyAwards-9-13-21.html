Leslie Odom Jr. and Audra McDonald will present the Tony Awards

Hot Club Tracks Staff - Sept 13th, 2021

Tony and Grammy Award-winning singer and actor Leslie Odom Jr. will host a splashy TV special, while Tony, Grammy, and Emmy Award-winning actress and singer Audra McDonald will host the Tony Awards.

The telecast's producers revealed Monday that Odom will host the two-hour celebration of Broadway's comeback from 9 p.m. to 11 p.m. ET/PT on Sept. 26, while McDonald will host the award ceremony from 7 p.m. to 9 p.m. ET/PT.

The four-hour occasion will be broadcast live from Broadway’s Winter Cultivate Theater. Capping the evening will be the granting of the three best grants: best play, best play restoration and best melodic.

Broadway theaters unexpectedly closed on March 12, 2020, thumping out all appears — counting 16 that were still planned to open within the spring. The four-hour grants and extraordinary are an endeavor to celebrate live theater and serve as a splashy promotion that Broadway is creeping back to commonality.
