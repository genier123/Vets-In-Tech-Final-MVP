DJ Snake Reportedly Sabotaged During Ibiza Set — Fans Claim He Collapsed Backstage




Hot Club Tracks Staff: 2025-10-02


Ibiza’s legendary nightlife scene was shaken last night when DJ Snake’s headline set at Ushuaïa was abruptly cut short. Fans on social media claim the superstar DJ collapsed backstage after what insiders are calling a deliberate act of “equipment sabotage.”


What Happened On Stage

Witnesses describe the music cutting out multiple times before the lights flickered and Snake himself stormed off the stage mid-drop. Moments later, crew rushed out and signaled the crowd the show was over. Fans said the energy went from euphoric to chaotic in seconds.


Rumors of Sabotage

Anonymous sources close to the production allege that wires in his setup were tampered with hours before the performance. Some even suggest rival DJs had a hand in the chaos fueling a wildfire of conspiracy theories in the EDM community.


Health Concerns

Unconfirmed reports say DJ Snake collapsed from exhaustion or stress immediately after leaving the stage. His team has not issued a public statement, but photos circulating online show security escorting him out quickly with medics nearby.


Fan Reaction

Twitter, TikTok, and Instagram exploded overnight with hashtags like #PrayForSnake and #IbizaDrama. Some fans voiced concern for his health, while others are convinced this was all part of a staged promo for his rumored upcoming album.


What’s Next

- Will DJ Snake address the sabotage rumors directly?
- Will Ibiza venues tighten security around headline acts?
- Could this ignite new rivalries in the already competitive EDM world?
- And was this really an attack or just a freak tech failure amplified by gossip?


Bottom Line

Whether the Ibiza collapse was sabotage, stress, or something in between, it’s the kind of story that has the music world buzzing. Until DJ Snake speaks out, speculation will only keep building and fans around the globe are waiting for answers.
