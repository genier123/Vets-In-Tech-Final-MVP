Tips for Creating Your Halloween 2025 DJ Set

  1. Mix genres: Alternate between high-energy EDM, spooky house, and classic hits to maintain energy.
  2. Use seasonal remixes: Many DJs release Halloween-specific edits each year.
  3. Incorporate samples: Add screams, creepy FX, or horror movie quotes.
  4. Build tension: Start slow, ramp up energy, then cool down for eerie moments.
  5. Check key and BPM: Smooth transitions keep the crowd immersed.
