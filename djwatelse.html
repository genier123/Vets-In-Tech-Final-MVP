Biography of DJ Was Else




Early Life and Musical Influences

DJ Was Else, from Upstate, NY, grew up surrounded by a rich tapestry of musical genres—from hip-hop to world music. Captivated by the underground music scene, he honed his craft at local clubs and raves, igniting a passion for DJing and music production that would define his career.


Rise to Prominence

In 2004, DJ Was Else began to establish himself in the local music scene, known for his eclectic mixes and ability to engage audiences. His breakthrough came with the release of his single "Bounce That" in 2018, which showcased his unique blend of house and world sounds. This success opened doors to performances at prominent festivals and clubs across the country.


Hot Club Tracks and PG Digital.co

In 2020, DJ Was Else launched the Hot Club Tracks music and entertainment blog, a platform dedicated to celebrating the vibrant world music scene. The blog highlights emerging talent, features in-depth interviews, music reviews, and industry insights, reflecting his commitment to fostering community and supporting fellow artists.


Additionally, he is the founder of PG Digital.co, a digital agency that focuses on helping musicians and creatives build their online presence. Through PG Digital.co, DJ Was Else empowers artists to navigate the ever-evolving digital landscape, providing resources and strategies to enhance their reach and impact.


Wat Else Radio Mix-Show and Notable Appearances

DJ Was Else hosts the Wat Else Radio Mix-Show, where he curates and showcases the best in world and open-format music. The show features guest mixes, interviews with fellow artists, and exclusive tracks, making it a go-to destination for music lovers.


He has also made a notable guest appearance on Pitbull’s Puro Pari Mix on Sirius XM Radio, further elevating his profile and connecting with a broader audience. This exposure highlights his versatility and appeal in the music industry.


Musical Style and Collaborations

DJ Was Else’s sound is a dynamic fusion of house, world, and open-format music, appealing to a diverse audience. His collaborations with fellow artists and producers have led to innovative tracks that push the boundaries of music. His work continues to receive acclaim, solidifying his reputation as a trailblazer in the industry.


Current Endeavors

As of 2024, DJ Was Else is focused on new music and expanding the reach of Hot Club Tracks and PG Digital.co, ensuring that his influence in the music scene continues to grow.


Conclusion

DJ Was Else is not just a talented DJ and producer; he is a visionary actively shaping the future of music across genres. With his innovative projects and unwavering dedication to the community, he is poised to make an indelible mark on the industry for years to come.
